# 14.5.2.2. 동작 변경키(Key modifiers)

<a id="14-04-12-02-s1"></a>

## 1. Ctrl(⌘)
Toggles between Design and Edit.

<a id="14-04-12-02-s2"></a>

## 2. Alt(⌥)
Toggles between Design and Move.

<a id="14-04-12-02-s3"></a>

## 3. Shift(⇧)
옵션 설명이 정의된 뒤에 여러가지 기능을 설명해보자.
